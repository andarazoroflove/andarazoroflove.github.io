this is a collection of the essentials. if you plan on learning to encode VCD, SVCD, or DivX;-) movies you will need at least these following items. i have made it easy for you and zipped them all up...just extract them to a directory and run the executables!

DivXCalc

DVD2AVi

FlasKMPeG

SmartRipper

TMPGEnc 1.2e

VirtualDub

VCDGear 2.0

in addition to these essential tools, i would recommend picking up the DivX;-) codec, the Radium MP3 codec, and maybe the nAVI dec (the SMR is not a codec as it only allows for DECompression)

another great idea is reading over the literature at doom9.net, vcdhelper.com, divx-digest.com, and nicky pages digital solutions
